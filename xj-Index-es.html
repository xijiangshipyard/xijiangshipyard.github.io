Independent Game: Xijiang Shipyard

Xijiang Shipyard "is a creative game with a realistic art style that focuses on free creation.

There are numerous features that serve creation:
-- Diverse geometric shapes,
-- Rich realistic materials,
-- Realistic plants,
-- Diverse skills to accelerate construction,
-- Independent editors to carve details,
-- 3D printing interfaces to bring virtuality into reality, model interfaces to bring the world into other virtual worlds.
